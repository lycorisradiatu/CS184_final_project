Goals and Deliverables

What we are trying to accomplish?

We want to implement different remeshing methods including Isotropic remeshing and Anisotropic remeshing and also try alternate subdivision schemes like Butterfly scheme and Catmull-Clark.

What results are we going for?

We'd like to use same mesh to produce multiple images using different methods mentioned above to see the different effects these methods have on images. If have time, we might also want to increase the performace of remeshing and subdivision.

Why we think we can accomplish those goals?

We found multiple papers and slides that can help us with the different remeshing and subdivision methods. We believe with the help of course material and previous homework, we will be able to accomplish these goals.
