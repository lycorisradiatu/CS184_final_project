Modified Butterfly Subdivision Scheme

When we implement butterfly scheme, we computes a new scalar value for each edge midpoint of the triangulation with four distinguish cases.

Case 1: The edge connects two vertices of valence 6

Case 2: The edge connects a K-vertex (K != 6) and a 6-vertex

Case 3: The edge connects two extraordinary vertices

Case 4: Boundary Edges
